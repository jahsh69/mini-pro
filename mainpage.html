Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?

Signing up is easy! Click on the "Get Started" button and follow the instructions to create your account.

What is the cost of the service?

Our platform is free for students. For recruiters, we offer flexible pricing plans based on their needs.

How does resume analysis work?

Our AI-powered tool analyzes your resume and provides actionable feedback to improve its quality and effectiveness. It checks for key elements like formatting, keywords, and overall structure to ensure your resume stands out to recruiters.

Can I apply for jobs directly through the platform?

Yes, you can! Once you find a job listing that matches your skills, simply click the "Apply" button to submit your application directly through our platform.

How do I get job alerts?

You can enable job alerts in your account settings. We’ll notify you via email or in-app notifications whenever a new job matching your preferences is posted.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. All your data is encrypted and stored securely, and we comply with all relevant data protection regulations.
